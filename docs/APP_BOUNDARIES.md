# Application Boundaries
- Inventory & Procurement: items, suppliers, stock, counts, requisitions, POs, receiving, returns.
- Accounting: bills, AP, payments, GL, taxes, reconciliation, financial reports and postings.
- POS: customer orders, sales, payments, receipts, cashier sessions, voids and refunds.
- Staff & Payroll: employee identity, schedules, attendance, leave and payroll.
- Operations Command Center: operational requests, tasks, projects, approvals and management history.
